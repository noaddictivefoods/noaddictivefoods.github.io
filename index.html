No Addictive Foods Diet

The No Additive Foods Diet is based on a few key principles:

  • The US population continues to become more obese, despite more than ever being known about nutrition.
  • Food companies are incentivized to create food that is both cheap and highly consumable/palatable.
  • Much of what we know about what is healthy or not is right, but we're missing the ability to navigate a key pitfall.

The No Addictive Foods Diet is not actually adversly different or in opposition with any other popular diet. In fact, the diet itself is a framework that helps us view what is right about those diets, and is a way to navigate the pitfalls of the modern food environment.
